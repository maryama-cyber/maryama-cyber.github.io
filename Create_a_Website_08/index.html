My Home Is Not Here Anymore

Friday, February 16, 2018

We were supposed to be separated only for 3-4 months. He would come with the girls as soon as the orientation and preceptorship periods would finish. As soon as my residence card would be valid and I could invite them to come. During their stay, he would actively search for work - it would be easier once inside the country, we were told. He would start working and we would go back to normal life.

The Beginning Of All The Things That Would Go Wrong

Monday, December 1, 2017

I wasn't afraid. We would find a way to make it work. In a way I was excited of the new, but in the same time I was crying to leave my girls behind. It was supposed to be for only 4 months, tops. Little did I know. I wasn't even supposed to be the one to go work in the Middle East. It was his plan, from the beginning. He was supposed to leave first and we would follow. Fate would have it other way. I managed to get the job before him, so there went our original plan. Before we knew it, it was me who was packing up and saying good byes to my loved ones. Just 4 short months we promised to each other. We could do it!
